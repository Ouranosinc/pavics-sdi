sdm_csv Species distribution Model (GBIF-CSV table as input) (v0.10)

Indices preparation for SDM process

  • resource (application/x-netcdf, application/x-tar, application/zip) –

    NetCDF Files or archive (tar/zip) containing netCDF files. (Info)

  • gbif (string) – GBIF table (csv) with tree occurence (output of ‘GBIF data fetch’ process )
  • indices ({'CDD_AMJJAS', 'CFD_ONDJFM', 'FD_ONDJFM', 'FD_Apr', 'FD_May', 'FD_Jun', 'GD4_yr', 'ID_yr', 'PRCPTOT_yr', 'PRCPTOT_ONDJFM', ..}) – Climate indices related to growth conditions of tree species

  • output_gbif (image/png) – PNG graphic file showing the presence of tree species according to the CSV file

  • output_reference (application/x-tar, application/zip) – Archive (tar/zip) containing calculated climate indices
  • output_prediction (application/x-tar, application/zip) – Archive containing files of the predicted growth conditions
  • output_info (application/pdf) – Graphics and information of the learning statistics
  • output_log (text/plain) – Collected logs during process run.
