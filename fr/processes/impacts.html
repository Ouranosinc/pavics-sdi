class flyingpigeon.processes.SDMcsvProcess[source]¶

sdm_csv 0.10 – Species distribution Model (GBIF-CSV table as input)

Indices preparation for SDM process

Paramètres:
  • resource (application/x-netcdf) – NetCDF Files or archive (tar/zip) containing netCDF files.
  • gbif (string) – GBIF table (csv) with tree occurence (output of “GBIF data fetch” process )
  • input_indices ({'CDD_AMJJAS', 'CFD_ONDJFM', 'FD_ONDJFM', 'FD_Apr', 'FD_May', 'FD_Jun', 'GD4_yr', 'ID_yr', 'PRCPTOT_yr', 'PRCPTOT_ONDJFM', ..}) – Climate indices related to growth conditions of tree species
  • period ({'all', '1951-1980', '1961-1990', '1971-2000', '1981-2010'}) – Reference period for climate conditions (all = entire timeseries)
  • archive_format ({'zip', 'tar'}) – Result files will be compressed into archives. Choose an appropriate format
Retourne:

  • output_gbif (image/png) – PNG graphic file showing the presence of tree species according to the CSV file
  • output_PA (image/png) – PNG graphic file showing PA mask generated based on netCDF spatial increment
  • output_indices (application/x-tar) – Archive (tar/zip) containing calculated climate indices
  • output_reference (application/x-tar) – Archive (tar/zip) containing calculated climate indices
  • output_prediction (application/x-tar) – Archive containing files of the predicted growth conditions
  • output_info (application/pdf) – Graphics and information of the learning statistics
  • output_log (text/plain) – Collected logs during process run.
